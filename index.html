FAQ's

Frequently asked questions

01.
How long does it take to create my project?
The usual timeframe falls within the range of 2 to 5 weeks, depending on the completeness of the data you supply (such as text/copywriting, images, etc.) and the complexity of the project.
02.
What about the payment?
We split the payment into two installments. You'll make an initial payment of 50% after reviewing the design mock-up and giving the green light for the project to continue. The remaining 50% is scheduled for payment 14 days following the first installment.
03.
Can I pay the remaining payment when the project is about to be launched?
I apologize, but we cannot accommodate that request. In our experience, a project can occasionally take longer than expected, as we may still need to wait for you to prepare and provide us with descriptions, images, videos, and other materials. That's why, after the initial payment, we will continue to send invoices for payment every 14 days.
04.
What are the requirements for me to prepare?
Depending on the project's requirements, but usually, we need:
 
  • Company logo or branding identity
  • Address and contact information such as phone or email
  • Company description (profile, mission, vision, history, etc.)
  • Product or service descriptions and advantages
  • Product or service descriptions and advantages
  • Company location on Google Maps
  • Business legality documents (if using a .CO.ID domain - Indonesia only)
05.
If I need to build a website, do I need to purchase a domain and hosting on my own beforehand?
You don't have to. If you choose to have us create your website, you'll receive a complimentary domain and hosting. We'll take care of the domain and hosting registration. Additionally, we'll notify you about the renewal invoices for the domain and hosting in the upcoming years.
06.
What makes your service expensive?

Yes, some still wonder about this even though our offered price is already the most affordable in its class. Here's the explanation:

Our process demands time, meticulous planning, and thorough research. Unlike some other design services, we AVOID USING TEMPLATES. We craft your projects from the ground up, guided by thoughtful planning and extensive research. Even the choice of fonts is carefully weighed, all in the pursuit of delivering a distinctive design that enhances your business.

07.
Can I update or make modifications to my project by myself?"
Certainly. We will send you the raw file of the project (.ai, .ae, .psd, .fig, etc). If you trust us with your website development, we utilize WordPress, making it convenient for you to independently edit website content without any additional charges. Once the website is live, we will provide you with a guide on how to edit website content in PDF format.
08.
How long does it take for my website to reach #1 on Google search?
Some of our clients take 2-3 months to achieve the #1 ranking on Google. However, there's no guarantee that your website will also attain the #1 ranking for the targeted keywords. Some of your competitors may have substantial marketing budgets or have been established in the field for years. Nevertheless, we ensure that your website is SEO-friendly right from the start.
09.
Where is Renea Studio located?
We are a website development service based in Lumajang, a small city in East Java that is often referred to as the 'Austria van Java' due to its stunning natural landscapes. Despite our location, we have created websites for numerous clients from Indonesia and all over the world. Contact us to kickstart the creation of your website.
